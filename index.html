About Us

Glorifying God in Ministry and Mission

Mapes Baptist Church is a beacon of faith and community nestled in the serene countryside of Vanderhoof, BC. Located on the picturesque Blackwater Road, our church stands as a testament to the unwavering spirit of worship and fellowship.

We exist to change hearts and change lives. Through the teachings of the Bible and the power of community, we inspire transformation that leads to a more purposeful and spiritually enriched life.

"But the fruit of the Spirit is love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, self-control; against such things there is no law." — Galatians 5:22-23

Whether you are a long-time resident or just passing through, you are warmly invited to join us for a service, a prayer, or even just a quiet moment of reflection.

Our Ministries

There is a place for everyone at Mapes Baptist Church. Find where God is calling you to serve and grow.

Children's Ministry

Dedicated age-appropriate teaching in a loving and welcoming environment. Children grow in faith through song, story, and fellowship.

Shoebox Ministry

Each year Mapes Church packs shoeboxes for needy children around the world — a tangible expression of God's love across borders.

Prayer & Praise

We believe in the power of prayer. Submit your prayer requests and praise reports — our community stands with you.

Our Pastor

Pastor Ron DeMoss

Lead Pastor

Pastor Ron DeMoss has faithfully led the Mapes Baptist Church family for over 13 years alongside his beloved wife Linda, who went to be with the Lord on July 27, 2025. Together, they built a legacy of grace, compassion, and unwavering dedication to God's Word.

Pastor Ron continues that mission today, bringing heartfelt teaching and pastoral care rooted in Scripture while guiding us through the challenges of life with hope and faith.
