Ephesians 2 Pastor Ron

Dead to Sin, Alive in Christ

Pastor Ron teaches that we are all dead in trespasses and sin until we trust Jesus Christ as our personal Lord and Savior. He emphasizes remembering our past life before Christ and how God's mercy and love have changed us. Themes include being made alive together with Christ by grace, seated with Him in heavenly places, and being built together into a dwelling place for God.

Matthew 13 Jed

Can We Walk Away from God?

Jed teaches that Jesus' parables in Matthew 13 explain the mysteries of the kingdom of heaven. He explains four parables — The Sower, The Weeds among the Wheat, The Mustard Seed, and The Leaven — each illustrating a different aspect of God's kingdom. God gives people many chances to respond to His call, but ultimately each individual must choose whether to follow Him or walk away.

Romans 8:1-11 Dave Ashcroft

The Pivot Point: Life in the Spirit

Dave Ashcroft delivers a sermon on Romans 8:1-11, which he describes as the "pivot point" for transitioning from a past lived for the flesh to a future lived for the Spirit. While Romans 7 illustrates the believer's struggle with sinful nature, Romans 8 offers the solution — life in the Spirit.
